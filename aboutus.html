Volta Mobiles was founded in Paris in 2015 by the two brothers Otxo and Mario Conti.
Right from the start, the specialization was in the design and production of ingenious mobiles and stables
- naturally strongly influenced by the creator of the very first mobiles, the artist Alexander Calder.
In 2017 the workshop was moved south to Barcelona.
Since we started in 2019, we have had this very smart company in our program. And it gives us great pleasure to work with the entire team.

These Volta mobiles are easy objects to put on the desk,
Not only does it give off the feeling of Nordic vintage props just by "putting it on,"
It is a prop that melts overwhelmingly well in a classical and modern space.
The Bauhaus-style classic and simple color combination blends over angled lines and round mobile pieces to add a modern feel.
It's a mobile function, so it moves slowly when there's air flow, but when you look at it, it gives you emotional stability
It is recommended to leave it on the bedside, or decorate it on tea tables, study, etc.

“The idea of creating a mobile had been in my head for a long time. I simply decided to make it myself!”

PARIS,

THE EMBLEMATIC MODEL OF THE VOLTA

Its palette of primary colors, inspired by the De Stijl movement - of which Piet Mondrian was a part - will enhance your interior thanks to its resolutely modern spirit.

All mobiles are handmade from reused metal and aluminum, so the products are all produced sustainably.
